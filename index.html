About Me

I'm Theviru Randira, a passionate Graphic Designer and Social Media Strategist with over 5 years of experience crafting visual stories that captivate and convert. My journey in design has taken me through diverse industries, giving me a unique perspective on what makes brands truly stand out.

I've had the privilege of working with leading e-money platforms, where I designed user-friendly interfaces and marketing materials that simplified complex financial concepts. Additionally, my experience managing social media agencies has honed my ability to create content that not only looks stunning but drives real engagement and results.

At Randira Designs, I believe that great design is more than just aesthetics—it's about solving problems, telling stories, and creating meaningful connections between brands and their audiences.
